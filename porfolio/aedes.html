    • INTERIOR & GRAPHIC DESIGN

    Aedes

    Aedes is my thesis project carried out to conclude my course in Interior Design at the Politecnico di Milano. It is a project for a former church in Varese, which becomes a crossroads, a campsite, a meeting place. Aedes is intended to represent the union of all these aspects in a single space, in which to live as a community, stop for a short break or spend an adventurous day. The structures I and my team have designed are based on the archetype of the forest: the image of the jungle led us to design devices in the shape of trees, in the trunks of which users can find shelter and build their own beds. These devices are then connected by a variety of elements painted with fluorescent paint, which will make them glow at night.

    In this project I used Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Indesign, Autocad and 3ds Studio Max.

    BOOKING APP

    In addition to the various devices, I also designed an app that users could use to book their stay and then use the tools provided.
