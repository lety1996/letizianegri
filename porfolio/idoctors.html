    • UX/UI DESIGN

    iDoctors

    iDoctor is an online service that offers the possibility to book, quickly and conveniently, any health service on their preferred days, times and areas of the city. Its aim is to facilitate the doctor-patient encounter avoiding loss of time, waiting and appointments made at times that are problematic for the patients.
    For this service I studied the UX of the site, both desktop and mobile, checking its usability, and then designed and proposed a new UI, going from creating new wireframes to making a prototype.

    In this project I used Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator and Figma.

    Desktop Mobile

    1 - Discovery Phase

    AS IS ANALYSIS

    During the first phase of analysis of the website, in both its desktop and mobile versions, I started with an assessment of the usability of the website itself, following the "Jakob Nielsen's 10 Heuristics".

    • LEARNABILITY: the website is easy to use and the goal easily reached thanks to the search bar immediately on the home page, it is clear to the user how to find a specialist in their city; it is also intuitive learn to navigate between the various sections.

    • EFFICIENCY: the process of booking an appointment is quick and easy while the research process is, in some cases, long and repetitive. The video consulting booking service is not very different from the other booking process and that can be confusing.

    • MEMORABILITY: the user who returns to use the site could be facilitated by adding the possibility of storing favourites and searches, but given its ease of use, the user can easily return to using it with the same ability after a period of time.

    • ERRORS: errors are easily managed and prevented, however it would be useful to implement a FAQs section.

    • SATISFACTION: the website provides all the informations necessary to reach the goal of booking a medical appointment in an informed manner and it is pleasant to use.

    In conclusion the website is usable.

    However, some aspects should be improved, such as: the coherence of the icons and illustrations system, the names of every steps in the search phase and implement implement the FAQs section or a live chat.

    TARGET

    The last part of this first phase was the identification of the target audience, done through user reviews and tools such as Semrush, and the creation of a survey to propose to potential users.
    The target audience defined for the website have this characteristics:
    - any gender or social background;
    - live in italy;
    - age between 18 and 54;
    - mobile device users.

    COMPETITORS

    I went on to identify and collect information about iDoctors' competitors, sites that offering a similar service, and then comparing their characteristics.
    Thanks to this it was possible to find ideas to improve the user experience of the website, such as the possibility of saving favourites bookmarks and allowing user customisation.

    SURVEY

    Taking into account all the analysis carried out so far I have decided to create a 10-question survey to understand:
    - the habits when people have to book a medical service and if this kind of service (not necessarily iDoctors) is known;
    - what influences people to use this service;
    - what are the factors that could influence the choice of a specialist.

    The target group for this type of analysis were 63 people over the age of 18, not necessarily users of the platform or other similar services.)

    RESULTS:
    - users often use websites to book services or a medical visit, in this way they can book that in the shortest possible time;
    - even users who have never used this type of service know it and would use it, but some are held back by knowledge of digital means;
    - it is very important for the users to choose by comparing the reviews of other patients;
    - in order to make an informed choice, the users want to be able to know the profile of the doctor and have information about their specialisations;
    - it's important to have the possibility of a conversation with the doctor before the visit.

    2 - Wireframes Phase

    After analysing the opportunities that emerged during the Discovery phase I designed 5 wireframes, inserting and modifying some elements, for improve the user experience of the iDoctors' website.

    HOMEPAGE - DESKTOP

    I made the Homepage simpler by removing elements that made the process slow and confusing. In the header the search function remains, present on every page, the link to the "You are a doctor" section and the drop-down menu to access your account and view saved specialists, booking history, etc. Then we can find:
    - the search bar, which allows you to find results only for video consultations or both, and a list of the most requested specialisations, to be able to immediately access the relevant doctors;
    - the main information on how the site works;
    - user reviews of the service received.

    RESEARCH RESULTS - DESKTOP

    Here we find a list of different specialists with a map showing the locations of the different clinics. The results can be filtered according to certainparameters, including the available date and insurance held, and also sort them according to reviews, price and distance. In each "doctor's card" we find details about the doctor and a user review, then we can continue reading the doctor's profile by clicking on "View profile" or select the desired date and time and proceed with the booking immediately.

    DOCTOR'S PROFILE - DESKTOP

    In the doctor's profile page we find the resume, organized in section that the user can expand; I decided to treat this part in this way because in the beginning it was long and full of information, while grouping and hiding the parts that the user is not interested in at that moment, everything is clearer and easier to read.
    After this part we find the reviews of other users who have previously visited by the doctor. At any time the user can book the service he need in the desired date and time.

    PATOLOGY INFORMATION - DESKTOP

    This page also contained a lot of information and it was long and difficult to read, so I used the same treatment as on the previous page: by grouping all the information into sections that the user can expand or reduce when necessary.

    SAVED SPECIALISTS - DESKTOP

    The "Favourites" section does not exist on the current site, so I decided to add it to allow the user to save some doctors and book a visit with them later. We can access it from the drop-down menu that appears by clicking on the profile image and from here we can book the service or even remove the doctor from the list.

    HOMEPAGE - MOBILE

    In the mobile version of the homepage, I have maintained the same elements as in the desktop version:
    - the navigation bar has been reduced, we find a hamburger menu immediately to the left and on the right the icon which allows, from any part of the site, to edit or make a new search;
    - as the first thing there is a search box from which it is possible to search for the desired visit. The mobile version also offers the possibility to search by type of visit;
    - then there is all the information on how the service works and the reviews from other users.

    RESEARCH RESULTS - MOBILE

    Here we find a list of different specialists, the map with the location of the different doctors can be viewed by clicking on the relative link; the results can be filtered and sorted according to need.
    In the "doctor card" we can find, in addition to the doctor's details and a review, the first available day fot the appointment, while for the other dates and times the user have to click on "More", then a pop-up will open.

    DOCTOR'S PROFILE - MOBILE

    This section has the same elements of the desktop version: we can find the informations about the doctor and the possibility to book an appointment in every time.

    PATOLOGY INFORMATION - MOBILE

    Here we can find the informations about a specific patology, grouped for riduced space when they are not necessary.

    SAVED SPECIALISTS - MOBILE

    In this section there are the doctors that the user saved for booking an appointment later.
