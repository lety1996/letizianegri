    WHO I AM

    About Me

    In 2019 I graduated in Interior Design at Politecnico di Milano: during this experience I was able to discover different aspects of design, being more interested in the graphic part of the projects. At the end of my studies I started working as a Junior Designer in Publicis Sapient, and then in LifeGate, where I had the opportunity to grow professionally, thanks to the stimulating and dynamic environment and the period spent, as an agency consultant, in the Design Studio team of Vodafone Italia.
    During part of my free time I'm a volunteer rescuer at Croce Banca Milano, and I look after the social and offline communication of the section I belong to.I always like to learn new things and test myself with new challenges, for this reason I'm part of the TEDxUNICATT's team where manage the creative&IT area.
